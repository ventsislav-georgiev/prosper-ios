App Review notes — Prosper Remote Terminal

WHAT THE APP DOES
Prosper attaches to terminal sessions running on the user's own Mac (which runs
the companion Prosper desktop app) and streams them to iPhone/iPad. The phone and
Mac communicate over the user's private Tailscale network — there is no account,
no login, and no backend service operated by us. Terminal traffic flows directly
device-to-Mac.

HOW TO REVIEW WITHOUT A MAC OR TAILSCALE
You do not need any external setup. The app ships with a fully offline DEMO:

  1. Launch the app. Tap "Remote Terminal".
  2. On the "Machines" screen, tap "Try the demo" (bottom of the list).
  3. Tap the "Demo session". A sample terminal session opens and is fully
     interactive — the on-screen keyboard and shortcut bar work; typed text is
     echoed. (No real shell runs on-device; iOS sandboxing prevents that, which
     is exactly why real sessions are hosted on the user's Mac.)

The "?" button (top-right on the home and Machines screens) opens an in-app
"How it works" guide explaining the Prosper + Tailscale model.

CONNECTIVITY
The "Connect" field expects the user's own Mac's Tailscale hostname or IP. A
reviewer not on the user's tailnet cannot and is not expected to reach a real
machine — please use the built-in demo to evaluate functionality.

ENCRYPTION
Uses only standard/exempt encryption (no proprietary crypto).
