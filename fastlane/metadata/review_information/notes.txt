App Review notes — Prosper Remote Terminal

WHAT THE APP DOES
Prosper attaches to terminal sessions running on the user's own Mac (which runs
the companion Prosper desktop app) and streams them to iPhone/iPad. The phone and
Mac communicate over the user's private Tailscale network — terminal traffic flows
directly device-to-Mac and does not pass through any service we operate.

An OPTIONAL feature, "Remote Wake", lets a signed-in user wake their own sleeping
Mac. This is the ONLY feature that requires an account; everything else (terminal,
demo) works fully signed-out.

HOW TO REVIEW WITHOUT A MAC OR TAILSCALE
You do not need any external setup, and you do NOT need to sign in. The app ships
with a fully offline DEMO:

  1. Launch the app. Tap "Remote Terminal".
  2. On the "Machines" screen, tap "Try the demo" (bottom of the list).
  3. Tap the "Demo session". A sample terminal session opens and is fully
     interactive — the on-screen keyboard and shortcut bar work; typed text is
     echoed. (No real shell runs on-device; iOS sandboxing prevents that, which
     is exactly why real sessions are hosted on the user's Mac.)

The "?" button (top-right on the home and Machines screens) opens an in-app
"How it works" guide explaining the Prosper + Tailscale model.

ABOUT THE OPTIONAL "WAKE" BUTTON
The Wake button only appears when the app actually FAILS to reach a real, saved
Mac (i.e. that Mac is asleep/unreachable). In a review environment there is no
real Mac on your tailnet, so this surface is not reachable through normal use and
is EXPECTED to be non-functional. Wake requires signing in (passwordless email
magic link) and only ever wakes a Mac the same account owns. The terminal and
demo do not depend on it.

ACCOUNTS / SIGN IN
Sign-in is passwordless: the user enters an email address and taps a one-time
magic link. We do not use third-party sign-in, and "Sign in with Apple" is not
required here because we do not offer any third-party/social login option
(Guideline 4.8 does not apply — email is our only login mechanism and is used
solely to operate the optional Remote Wake feature).

ACCOUNT DELETION
A signed-in user can delete their account in-app: Settings → Delete Account. This
permanently removes their account and remote-wake data from our server and signs
the device out.

ENCRYPTION
Uses only standard/exempt encryption (standard TLS, no proprietary crypto).
