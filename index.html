Privacy Policy — Prosper Remote

Last updated: 2026-06-22

Prosper Remote (“the app”) is designed to collect no personal data.

What we collect

Nothing. The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs. We operate no servers that your data passes through.

Data stored on your device

Machine addresses you type (your Mac’s Tailscale name or IP) and recent connection history are stored locally on your device only, so you don’t have to retype them. They are never transmitted to us and never leave your device except to connect to the machine you chose.

Terminal traffic

When you connect to your Mac, terminal input and output flow directly between your device and your Mac over your private Tailscale network. This traffic does not pass through any service operated by us — we cannot see it.

Tailscale

Connections rely on Tailscale, a third-party network. Your use of Tailscale is governed by Tailscale’s own privacy policy: tailscale.com/privacy-policy.

Children

The app is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
